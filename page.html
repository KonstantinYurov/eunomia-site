Julia Kim – von den Dreisch

Information

Julia graduated from Russian State University of Cinema (VGIK) in 2003, Master Degree: History of Cinema.

Julia started as a media event producer in Moscow working with filmmakers such as Francis Ford Coppola, Tom Tykwer and others. Produced shooting of the video interviews from the film festivals in Cannes, Berlin, Veniece, Locarno, and also produced shooting of the documentary tele-films for korean television companies KMB, MBC. Julia led the photo- and video shooting department in Tashkeel Studio, Dubai. Оulia was the head SMM manager in Bolshoi theater.

Julia is a member of the jury of international independent film festivals in Busan, Abu Dhabi, North Cyprus and Vladivostok.

Eugeniy Schastnyi

Information

Eugeniy Schastnyi, producer (Germany, Berlin) Currently producing a feature documentary dedicated to Konstantin Stanislavsky.

Eugeniy started as a producer working with filmmakers such as Francis Ford Coppola, Tom Tykwer, the Wachovskys, Sarah Jessika Parker, Quentin Tarantino, to name but a few.

In 2020 he produced a feature documentary “What she said: the art of Pauline Kael” directed by Rod Garver.

In addition to documentaries and educational projects, Eugeniy produces feature films about Pauline Kael “Movie maker” directed by Tarantino.
